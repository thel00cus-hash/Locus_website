About The Locus Education

The Locus Education was founded by Ajit Singh, a technical enthusiast and M.Tech Computer Science engineer with over 18 years of teaching experience in Engineering and Technology. He completed his M.Tech from Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, Bhopal in 2013, specializing in Computer Technology and Applications.

At The Locus Education, we provide a completely practical-oriented and engaging learning environment for students through online mode, helping them gain real-world skills and confidence at an affordable price.
